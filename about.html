Get to know me!

Hi! I'm Shaqib Habib, and I am a Full Stack Web Developer.

Throughout my two years in the tech industry working in event planning, community operations, and web development, I have developed a passion for project management and problem-solving, leading me to pursue a career in full-stack web development. I am committed to developing and creating strategic projects and enjoy exploring new ideas and programming languages. Having worked in a wide variety of environments, I’ve shown initiative and functioned cross-collaboratively to successfully achieve project goals while remaining calm under high-pressure deadlines and urgent client requests. Thorough attention to detail is crucial in identifying and analyzing to break down the big picture vision into manageable steps.

Currently, my goal is to work as a web developer for an organization I’m passionate about in an environment that fosters growth, development, and encourages creative freedom.

I wasn't always a computer programmer. After graduating from Indiana University, Bloomington in 2012, I set my heart out to work in the entertainment industry. I started out as an intern for an management company and another at an agency. Though two sides of the same coin, I delt with managing the actors and scheduling their daily meetings with my boss or assisting with submitting talent for potential film, television, or commerical roles. Even though I was enjoying the work lifestyle, I believe I needed something more in life.

After a year interning, I took a break to travel the world and learned to become more independent. Along my journey, I discovered skills I needed to tap into more. I was networking, communicating with different types of people from all different religions and lifestyles, and learned to enjoy life rather than worrying about what others had to say about me.

With two more years of growth, I grew a thick skin and headed back to Los Angeles. For the past five years, I've grown more as an individual. I've helped build communities, worked in the entertainment industry meeting celebrities and making people happy, and learned that a career makes the person they are if they put their mind to it. I worked in the entertainment industry on both the agency, management, and production side and I learned these skills to switch careers to feed my appitite which lead me down a development path.
